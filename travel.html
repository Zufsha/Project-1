Norway

A little about Norway

The western half of the Scandinavian peninsula is occupied by the northern European nation of Norway. The capital city of Oslo and the region to its extreme south are home to about half of the nation's population. Norway is made up of around two-thirds mountains, and some 50,000 islands are scattered along its heavily indented coastline, off deep glacial fjords.
